ABOUT ME
Designing with Intention,
Every Single Time

Hi, I'm Chompoo — a UX/UI Designer based in Bangkok, Thailand. I graduated from King Mongkut's University of Technology Thonburi (KMUTT) with a Bachelor of Science in Applied Computer Science–Multimedia, achieving a GPAX of 3.83 (First Class Honours).

With experience spanning startups, internships, and a current full-time role at Tech Invent Co., Ltd., I specialise in designing UX/UI that balances aesthetics with real-world usability — from enterprise web platforms to consumer mobile apps.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring motion graphics, editorial design trends, and refining my personal visual language.

HOW I WORK
A Process Built on
Care & Curiosity
01
Research & Discover
Understand users, problems, and business context through interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis.
02
Define & Ideate
Shape problem statements, build user journeys, and ideate solutions with structured creative thinking.
03
Design & Prototype
Create wireframes, high-fidelity visuals, and interactive prototypes that communicate the real experience.
04
Test & Iterate
Test with real users, analyse findings, and iterate — repeating until the experience feels just right.
