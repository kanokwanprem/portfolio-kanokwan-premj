ABOUT ME
Designing with Intention,
Every Single Time

Hi, I'm Chompoo — a UX/UI Designer based in Bangkok, Thailand. I graduated from KMUTT with a First Class Honours degree in Applied Computer Science (Multimedia).

With experience across startups and tech companies, I specialise in designing UX/UI that balances aesthetics with real-world usability. Currently at Tech Invent Co., Ltd., I bring a detail-oriented, user-centred approach to every project.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring editorial design trends and refining my personal visual language.
